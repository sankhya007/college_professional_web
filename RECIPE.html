Very Incredible Basil Sauce

11 Ingredients

40 Minutes

140 Calories

Ingredients


  • 1/2 cup Crisco Butter Flavor All-Vegetable Shortening
  • 1/2 cup Smucker's Natural Creamy Butter (stirred)
  • 1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 Tbsp. milk
  • 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 3/4 cups Pillsbury BEST All Purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • sugar

Directions

STEP 1

Preheat the oven to 350 degree.

STEP 2

In a large mixing bowl beat the shortening and peanut butter with an electric mixer on medium speed 30 seconds.

STEP 3

Add the 1/2 cup granulated sugar, brown sugar, baking powder, and baking soda. Beat until combined, scraping the sides of bowl.

STEP 4

Beat in egg, milk, and vanilla until combined. Slowly stir in the flour.

STEP 5

Shape dough into 1-inch balls.

STEP 6

Place 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet.

STEP 7

Bake in oven for 10 to 12 minutes or until edges are firm and bottoms are lightly browned.

STEP 8

Immediately press a chocolate kiss into each cookies center. Transfer cookies to a wire rack; let cool
