FRENCH DELIGHT

10 Ingredients

30 Minutes

180 Calories

Ingredients


  • 20 oz. chocolate chips (we used Enjoy LIfe, Vegan& Paleo chocolate chips)
  • 1 cup coconut milk (canned, recommend Native Forest)
  • 1/4 cup coconut sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp. vanilla
  • 1 Tbsp. coconut oil
  • 1 cup crunchy peanut butter

Directions

STEP 1

Add chocolate chips, coconut milk, sugar, and salt to a heavy saucepan, stir to combine.

STEP 2

Over low heat, stir until chocolate chips are completely melted.

STEP 3

Add the 1/2 cup granulated sugar, brown sugar, baking powder, and baking soda. Beat until combined, scraping the sides of bowl.

STEP 4

Beat in egg, milk, and vanilla until combined. Slowly stir in the flour.

STEP 5

Add vanilla and coconut oil, stir to incorporate. Spread fudge into a parchment-lined 8x8-inch pan.

STEP 6

Place 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet.

STEP 7

Bake in oven for 10 to 12 minutes or until edges are firm and bottoms are lightly browned.
