CHALER PAYESH

8 Ingredients

45 Minutes

150 Calories

Ingredients


  • 3 Tbsp. rice
  • 2 tsp. ghee
  • 1 liter full cream milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 12 cashew nuts
  • 12 pistachios
  • 12 almonds

Directions

STEP 1

Rice soaked in water. Drain the water and mix the rice with 2 teaspoon ghee. Set aside.

STEP 2

Rice and ghee added to a bowl. Rice and ghee mixed together.

STEP 3

Heat 1 liter of whole milk in a heavy bottom pan over medium-high heat and bring it to a boil.

STEP 4

Milk and ghee added to a pan. Once the milk comes to a boil, reduce the heat to low.

STEP 5

Add the rice to the pan and cook until the rice is nicely cooked and the payesh has thickened (35-40 minutes). Stir frequently while cooking.

STEP 6

Rice mixed with ghee added to the pan. Add ¼ cup of white granulated sugar and cook for another 3-4 minutes.

STEP 7

Sugar added to the pan. Add ½ teaspoon cardamom powder and mix well.

STEP 8

cardamom powder added to the pan. Check for sugar and add more if needed.

STEP 9

Give the deu fruits as layering.

STEP 10

Garnish and servr hot.
